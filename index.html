This is a compact voxel engine: terrain generation, chunks, greedy-ish face culling, block place/break, simple collisions.

Note: It's not a full Minecraft clone (no mobs, crafting, lighting engine, etc.) — but it's a solid foundation.

Tip: If right-click opens your browser menu, try again after the pointer is locked.
